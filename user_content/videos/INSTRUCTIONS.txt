
 VIDEO TESTING INSTRUCTIONS
============================

To test video/reel posting functionality:

1. ADD VIDEOS TO TEST:
    Place any cat videos (.mp4, .mov, .avi) in: user_content/videos/
   
2. VIDEO REQUIREMENTS:
   - Format: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM
   - Duration: 15-60 seconds (optimal for Instagram Reels)
   - Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical) or 1080x1080 (square)
   - Size: Under 100MB

3. TO RUN VIDEO TEST:
   python test_video_posting.py
